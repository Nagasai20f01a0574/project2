CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: 29 November 2025
Company: Project Kits (Hyderabad, India)

1. Cancellation

A user may request project cancellation before the work is fully completed. Cancellation must be requested through the official communication channel used during the project.

2. Refund Eligibility (Partial Refund Policy)

Project Kits provides partial refunds based on the work completed.

• If work has not started: Up to 80–90% refund (processing fee may apply).
• If work has partially started: Refund amount is reduced based on completed progress.
• If work is almost complete or delivered: Refund may not be provided.

This structure protects your team from unfair cancellations.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

No refund applies in the following situations:

• User delays causing missed academic deadlines
• User changes project requirements after confirmation
• User fails to provide required information on time
• Completed or delivered project files
• College rejects the project for reasons unrelated to your work

4. Delivery Issues

If you cannot complete a project for a genuine internal reason, a suitable refund will be issued at your discretion.

5. Policy Updates

These rules may change without prior notice.
Latest policy will always be available on the website.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when needed.
Changes will be posted on the website.
