PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: 29 November 2025
Company: Project Kits (Hyderabad, India)

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following through our website form and direct communication:

• Name
• Phone number
• Email
• Domain/area of the project

No payment data is collected on the website.

2. How We Use This Information

• Contact you regarding your project request.
• Understand your project requirements.
• Provide quotations, delivery timelines, and updates

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We store user information securely and restrict access to authorized team members only.
